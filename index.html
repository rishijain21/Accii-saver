Whats The Need of Acci-Saver?

Road accident is the most unwanted thing to happen to a road user, though they happen quite often. The most unfortunate thing is that we don't learn from our mistakes on road. Most of the road users are quite well aware of the general rules and safety measures while using roads but it is only the laxity on part of road users, which cause accidents and crashes. What is Acci-Saver?

Acci-Saver is a Combination of Hardware and Software which work togather in a way to save a Life.Its a complete Life Saving Solution

  • Reports Accident Faster to Nearby Police Stations
  • Sends Victims GPRS Location Automatically When triggered
  • Informs Your Loved Ones About your Accident Through Text.

Working of Acci-Saver

Acci-Saver is a Device which contain Arduino sensors Such as Shock Sensor , Burning Smell Detection Sensor, High Sound Frequency Sensor and a GPS Location Finder Sensor.

Acci-saver will be installed on the vehicle at maximum suspicious locations so that it can easily detect if any misshaping took place. Assuming any accident took place and the sensor detects, it will directly share the Vehicle location to the nearby Police station as well as Hospitals. Sending Requests from the sensor to the server is automated.
